Thank you, as always, for your continued partnership. We are delighted to be developing the Sakura Speak prototype. We are especially grateful for the clear product specification and the valuable vocabulary lists (107 pages in total) you provided.

This report summarizes everything implemented to date and shares the live URLs where you can try the product directly in your browser. Guided by the concept of a "serious, systematic learning platform" for Japanese learners abroad, we have prepared a hands-on experience that spans the full journey — from the public landing page all the way through the member area.

Most importantly, today we completed the integration of all categories of your Vocabulary Sheets — the centerpiece of your marketing. Learners can browse vocabulary by real-life scenes such as "supermarket," "bank," "airport," and "restaurant," and tap any word to hear its pronunciation.

The core user flow is up and running as a prototype: public LP, sign-up & subscription, member dashboard, lessons, mini-games, vocabulary, avatar, profile, community, review, news, and the admin console. Japanese/English language switching and mobile (smartphone) layouts are also supported.

Vocabulary Sheets — all categories complete today's main deliverable

Based on the vocabulary PDF you supplied, every word has been digitized with kanji, kana, romaji, and English. Because the Japanese text could not be machine-extracted from the source, each page was transcribed by hand to ensure accuracy. Learners pick a category, open a topic sheet, and tap a card to hear the pronunciation; a "Quiz mode" hides the meanings for self-testing.

Key features implemented

Public area (marketing LP)

  • Hero / About / Features / Curriculum (80 lessons, Class A–D) / 4 pricing plans / FAQ / CTA
  • The mascot "Meemi" hand-built in SVG (structured for easy swap once official art arrives)
  • Japanese / English language switching; smartphone support (including the hamburger menu)

Member area

  • Dashboard (progress, streak, coins, today's goals, news)
  • Lessons (immersive player screen, browser speech-synthesis pronunciation, multiple exercise types)
  • Three mini-games (timed quiz / memory match / listening) that auto-link to the vocabulary data
  • Vocabulary Sheets (completed today, above) / Review sheets / News reader
  • Meemi avatar customizer (parametric SVG, dress-up, save)
  • Profile & social (follow, etc.), Community (chat, voice rooms, events)

Foundation & operations

  • Auth screens (login / sign-up / email verification) and subscription (multi-currency, coupons, cancellation flow)
  • Admin CMS (role-based permissions; news, events, users, and report moderation)
  • Onboarding (first-run tutorial — a guided tour that highlights the real UI)
