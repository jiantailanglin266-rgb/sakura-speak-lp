Design & implementation — launch-ready, incl. the following:

  • Member dashboard, lessons & lesson player
  • Mini-games ×4 (quiz / memory / listening / sentence scramble)
  • Vocabulary Sheets — full data (10 cat / 101 topics / 2,122 words)
  • Preset avatars — several options built from your supplied images (selection only; no dress-up)
  • Profile & social, community, review sheets & news
  • Authentication & member accounts (real sign-up / login)
  • Member database & data persistence (e.g. Supabase)
  • Member-management screen & admin CMS
  • Subscription screen, ready for your Stripe connection
  • Onboarding tour & custom Meemi mascot (SVG)

To be provided / arranged by the client

  • Stripe — Stripe account/contract and the live payment integration (connecting checkout to real billing)
  • Backend service accounts — Supabase account/project (member data & auth; paid plan for production) and an email-sending service (SMTP, e.g. Resend/SendGrid) with a sending domain.
